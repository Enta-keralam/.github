# 🌴 Enta Keralam

**Experience Kerala Culture, Festivals & Traditions — All in One Place**

Enta Keralam is a platform dedicated to showcasing all cultural, religious, and traditional events happening across Kerala.

From temple festivals 🛕  
To mosque programs 🕌  
To church feasts ⛪  
To art forms like Kathakali & Theyyam 🎭  

We bring everything together in one unified platform for tourists, locals, and international visitors.

---

## 🌍 What We Offer

- 📅 Upcoming Events Calendar
- 🛕 Temple Festivals
- 🕌 Islamic Programs
- ⛪ Church Feasts
- 🎭 Cultural Performances
- 📍 Location & Travel Info
- 📸 Media & Highlights

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

To preserve and promote Kerala’s rich cultural and religious diversity in a modern, accessible digital platform.
